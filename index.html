Delete Your MyPetBook Account

If you would like to delete your MyPetBook account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

  • Open the MyPetBook app and log in with your account.
  • Go to More > Settings > Delete Account.
  • Confirm your decision to delete your account.
  • Once confirmed, the app will send a request to our server to delete your account using the following endpoint:
    https://auth-service.mypetbook.app/api/auth/delete-user/{userId}
  • All personal data including your profile, pets, appointments, and posts will be permanently deleted.
  • Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes as required.

The deletion process may take up to 7 days to fully complete. Once processed, your account cannot be recovered.

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team at support@mypetbook.com.
